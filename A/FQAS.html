Frequently Asked Questions

What is Perfumora? ▸
Perfumora is a premium perfume store offering a wide range of luxury and everyday fragrances for men and women.
Do you offer free delivery? ▸
Yes! We offer free delivery on all orders over Rs. 3000 all across Pakistan.
Can I return a perfume after opening it? ▸
Unfortunately, due to hygiene reasons, opened products cannot be returned. However, if the product is sealed and unused, you may return it within 7 days.
How long does shipping take? ▸
We usually deliver within 2-4 business days in major cities and 3-5 days in remote areas.
Do you sell testers or original perfumes? ▸
We only sell 100% original perfumes and high-quality testers at affordable prices.
